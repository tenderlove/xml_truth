Ruby Dee And The Snakehandlers - Genuine Roots and Country

Welcome to the Official Ruby Dee And The Snakehandlers website. We’re an americana/honkytonk/rockabilly outfit out of the Pacific NW with a couple records out under the Dionysus Records imprint. Check out our latest tour dates, news, listen to some music, browse our catalog, read our tour blog, and of course contact us to drop a line or book a show. Don’t forget to sign up for our RSS feed, and register for the site to leave comments.

Thanks for stopping by.
