March 26, 2009

New Green Tune!

We recorded a new song last night (3/24/09).  It's called- Green New Year.  It's about the environment and what we can do to help things stay green.  I'll post it once it's mixed.  It features GR veteran Michelle Zink, and newcomer Anna Ventor.  Stay tuned!



Thank You to Oakland GS!

We had a great concert this past Saturday night at the Valley Middle School in Oakland, NJ, thanks to an enthusiastic crowd of Oakland Girl Scouts. 

There was lots of dancing, cheering, and an overall fun vibe running through the air.  Very positive energy.  (I'll be writing more about this concert in the next day or two)

We hope to go back there again one day.

Thanks Oakland!

PS:  If anyone has any photos from that concert that they would be willing to share for the website, please contact:  dean@groovyrubyrocks.com 

I'd love to post them.
