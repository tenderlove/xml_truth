Welcome to the home of the 2008 Great Lakes Ruby Bash, the non-profit event where Rubyists and anyone interested in Ruby get together for talks, mingling, and hacking. Be sure to check out our blog for the latest information about the conference. Also be sure to download a copy of our brochure. It contains numerous bits of useful information.

Registration is open!

We have now opened registration for the Great Lakes Ruby Bash. The cost is $50, which includes lunch (if you registered by Tuesday, October 7th, 11:59pm) and the opportunity to hear great speakers. We'll see you there!
