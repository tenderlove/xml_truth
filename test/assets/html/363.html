ATTENTION ALL TEACHERS AND STUDENTS!
Ruby Bridges is developing a new book entitled Through Their Eyes, meaning a look at racism through the eyes of students.

If you are a student (or are the teacher of students and would like to introduce them to this project) and would like to participate, please write Ruby Bridges with thoughts on Racism and race relations in our country today.  If you’ve been touched by Ruby’s book, Through My Eyes or have had the opportunity to hear Ruby speak at your school, what were you views before and what are your views now? We’d love to hear your thoughts on these subjects - through your eyes! 

     Please send letters to:
      Ruby Bridges
      P.O. Box 870248
      New Orleans, LA 70187

Who knows? Maybe you’ll be lucky enough to have your letter published in this new book!


      Inside you will find a vast amount of information about Ruby
      Bridges' life and living legacy.
