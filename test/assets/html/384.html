Ruby Ranch Lodge is spacious and can host up to 200 guests. The Lodge boasts two huge event rooms, one with a beautiful rock fireplace, and the other with hand milled log accent walls and beautiful lighting. The Lodge also offers use of the commercial kitchen and dining area for guests. The kitchen has a pass through bar, which provides the perfect place for buffet style serving. The Lodge sleeps 20 overnight guests very comfortably.

The Lodge boasts two spacious event rooms, one with a beautiful rock fireplace and the other with hand milled log accent walls and beautiful lighting. The Lodge also offers limited use of a commercial kitchen and dining area for guests. The kitchen has a pass-through bar, which provides the perfect place for buffet style serving.  Available to our clients are enough tables to seat hundred twenty people and one hundred folding chairs. There is plenty of space to add additional rented tables and chairs if you so desire. You may hire a caterer of your choice or choose something off of our catering menu. You may also bring in your own alcohol; however, someone that is T.A.B.C. certified must serve the alcohol. 

The Lodge has enough overnight, accommodations for twenty to twenty eight people.
