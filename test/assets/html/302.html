 About the Shop
  • Children's Clothes Hip children’s clothes for babies up to size 8, featuring Bay Area local & independent artists: bold modern, vintage charming, witty urban.
  • Floral Design Lush arrangements of local-grown flowers for events, corporate spaces & special occasions. Same-day delivery to Oakland Children's Hospital, Kaiser Hospital Oakland, and Summit-Alta Bates Hospitals. Local delivery just $10 with $50 minimum.
  • Personal Shopping Service We'll help you choose a gift, then gift wrap and mail it for you. Not sure what to get the babies in your life? Gift Certificates allow you to leave the decision-making to them! Another way to give a gift that benefits local artists & entrepreneurs. Just call or email us to find out more.

MAMA'S NIGHT OUT: CHINESE NEW YEAR was super fun--if you missed it, you should pause now for a moment of regret...... Okay. Here's the dish: About 60 mamas came out Friday night for Chinese lantern cupcakes by Cake Ladies and dim sum yummies. Clearance prices on winter merchandise encouraged a little shopping between the cocktails and the free massage from local massage therapist Cathy Courtenay-Smith. But no one went home empty handed since Naturally Delicious Skincare made lucious little care packages, and our friend Sarah Young of 35th and Mac donated women's t-shirts we gave away with purchases.

ART AT RUBY'S GARDEN: WENDY YOSHIMURA We've been begging friend & neighbor Wendy Yoshimura to hang her gorgeous watercolor prints on our walls for months. Now her luminous works are on our walls, showing fruits and flowers that would light up your baby's room as well as your living or dining room. Prices range from $200-$525.
