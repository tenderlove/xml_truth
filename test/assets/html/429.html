You'll sense a special friendliness every time you stay at Big Rubys Key West. An island paradise where you are free from everyday pressures. A private guesthouse that allows you to renew your spirit in peace and in style.

Behind our doors you will discover magnificent palms towering over the inviting lagoon pool and the 15 person in ground Jacuzzi. There is a large sun deck and treetop walkway overlooking an exotic garden. The lounge has a big screen TV and there is a state of the art fitness center. All this is in an authentic traditional Conch style home setting.

Enjoy attentive service and pampering during your stay at Big Ruby's Guest House. You'll find it easy to meet people from all over the world and make friends you'll enjoy for years. We're large enough to offer you a stimulating variety of fellow guests, yet small enough to give you the personal attention and service which will make your stay memorable.

Start everyday with a complimentary chef prepared breakfast complimented by tropical fruits and juices. Exchange ideas for the day's activities and begin discovering new sights and experiences in paradise. Sailing, snorkeling or scuba diving, renting an on-site bicycle and absorbing the history of Key West or just laying by the pool, taking a refreshing dip whenever you please.
