Blue Moon Show Canceled

Posted in Nuz, Shows on December 7, 2007 by rubyshuz

Due to circumstances beyond anyone’s control, Ruby Shuz regretfully canceled our 12/6 Blue Moon appearance. This is our first time pulling out on a show, and we apologize to all concerned for any inconvenience.

The good news is that Ministry of Melodic Mayhem, featuring Ruby Shuz bassist Brandon Koeller, has agreed to take our place on the bill, saving the day as well as Christmas! It’s going to be a great show after all!
