Ruby Jane is your source for retro fabric, reproduction and quilting fabric, notions, books, embroidery transfers, heirloom patterns for quilts, handbags and wearables,  linens, bedding, aprons, pillows, bags, kitchenware, books and more from the  1860's to the 1960's. 

Featuring:
  • Premium quilt fabric from Moda, Marcus Brothers, Free Spirit, Michael Miller, Alexander Henry, Robert Kaufman, Timeless Treasures, Henry Glass and In the Beginning., Lyndhurst, Quilting Treasures, Red Rooster, RJR, Maywood Studos and more!
  • Sliced Bread reproduction 1930's tablecloths, napkins, aprons & dishtowels.
  • Historical costume and period dress patterns.
  • Quilt & craft patterns from Indygo Junction,  Favorite Things, and Amy Butler - Huge collection of apron patterns!
  • Baby gifts, placemats, growth charts, and stationery from Dolce Mia
  • Vintage Workshop Click & Craft CDs
