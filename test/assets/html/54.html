what's new?
 
  Well! Remember that ghost story with the ear tree that I’ve been telling you about for years? It’s finally coming to a library or independent bookstore near you! Actually, you can find The Secret of Laurel Oaks everywhere now. Albuquerque brother and sister, Gabe and Lila, visit a Louisiana plantation, where they stumble upon the secrets of a restless ghost named Daphne. She has wandered and haunted Laurel Oaks her entire deathtime, waiting for the right people to come along to prove that she didn’t murder those sweet little girls and their mother way back in the 1840s.

Or did she?
