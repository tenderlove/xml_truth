The historic Ruby Theatre was built in 1914 and named after the manager's step daughter Ruby Potter.

It has operated continuously as a movie house beginning with early silent films.

The theatre remains much like it was when the first movies were shown in Chelan over 85 years ago.

The Ruby is one of the last great small town theatres in the country, and is reminiscent of the good old days of theatre.
