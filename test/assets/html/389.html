Events

Boston Rubyists are living the dream: with at least three events scheduled per month, there's ample time to feed our ravenous need to learn and explore this great programming language.

Every 2nd Tuesday of the month, we have a lecture-style meeting. Every 1st and 3rd Tuesday of the month, we have hackfests, where we write code together.
