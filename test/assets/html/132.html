Flourish 2009: Open Source Conference

Flourish 2009 will be held April 3-4, 2009 at the University of Illinois at Chicago, 750 S. Halsted Street. The goal of the Flourish Conference is to promote the use and adoption of Free Libre Open Source Software (FLOSS) by demonstrating the practical applications of FLOSS in the business and academic world.

Attendees of Flourish have an opportunity to see how using, creating and supporting FLOSS software can enhance their careers, businesses and academic aspirations.

Visit http://www.flourishconf.com/ for details.
