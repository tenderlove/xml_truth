Ruby Users of Minnesota

Welcome

This is the home of Ruby Users of Minnesota — a fine group of Ruby enthusiasts from around the Twin Cities metro area, or pretty much wherever they feel like driving from.

You'll want to login or sign up to use any of the nifty features, like the help wanted , for-hire and projects sections.

To join the mailing list, just send mail to ruby.mn+subscribe@ruby.mn

Our group meets on the last Monday of each month at 7 PM (come early to hack Ruby code). Our meetings are held at:

Refactr

Eleven North-East Fourth Street
Suite 300
Minneapolis, MN 55413
Phone: 612-605-9740
